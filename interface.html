SNAKE GAME

Snake is a classic mobile game that became widely popular on early Nokia phones. Players control a snake that moves across the screen, collecting food to grow longer while avoiding collisions with walls or its own body. Its simple graphics and straightforward controls made it one of the most recognizable and iconic games in early mobile phone culture.
